Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our handmade earrings, shipping, care, and more.

Shipping & Orders

How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship within 1-2 business days and arrive within 3-5 business days via standard shipping. We also offer expedited 1-2 day shipping for urgent orders.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a flat $4.95 shipping fee.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we only ship within the United States, but we're working on international shipping options for the future!

Can I track my order?

Absolutely! You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also check your order status by contacting us with your order number.

What if my order is damaged in shipping?

We package everything carefully, but if your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos and we'll send a replacement right away at no cost to you.

Products & Materials

Are your earrings hypoallergenic?

Yes! All our ear wires are made from hypoallergenic materials including gold-plated, stainless steel, or sterling silver options. They're safe for sensitive ears.

What materials do you use?

We use sustainably sourced wood (primarily birch and walnut), eco-friendly acrylic paints, protective sealants, and hypoallergenic metal hardware.

How big are the earrings?

Sizes vary by design, but most of our earrings range from 0.5" to 2" in length. Specific dimensions are listed in each product description.

Are the earrings heavy?

No! Wood is naturally lightweight, so even our larger designs are comfortable for all-day wear. Most pairs weigh less than 0.3 oz.

Can I request custom colors or designs?

We love custom requests! Contact us with your ideas and we'll let you know if we can make it happen. Custom orders typically take 2-3 weeks.

Care & Maintenance

How should I clean my earrings?

Gently wipe with a soft, dry cloth after wearing. For deeper cleaning, use a slightly damp cloth and dry immediately. Avoid harsh chemicals or soaking in water.

Can I wear them in the shower?

We recommend removing your earrings before showering, swimming, or exercising to preserve the finish and prevent water damage.

How should I store my earrings?

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Individual jewelry pouches or compartments work best to prevent scratching.

Will the colors fade over time?

Our earrings are sealed with a protective coating to prevent fading. With proper care, colors should remain vibrant for years.

What if my earrings break?

While our earrings are durable, if something breaks due to a manufacturing defect within the first year, we'll replace it free of charge.

Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, return your earrings in original condition for a full refund.

How do I return an item?

Contact us first to get a return authorization number. Then ship the item back in its original packaging. We'll process your refund within 3-5 business days of receiving the return.

Who pays for return shipping?

For returns due to our error (wrong item, defect), we pay return shipping. For other returns, customers cover return shipping costs.

Can I exchange for a different style?

Yes! We're happy to exchange for a different style of equal or lesser value. If the new item costs more, just pay the difference.

What if I lose an earring?

Contact us! We often have single earrings available as replacements for a reduced cost, or we can offer a discount on a new pair.

General Questions

Are you a family business?

Yes! We're a small family-owned business based in the USA. Every order is hand-packed with love by our team.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Absolutely! Free gift wrapping is available at checkout, and you can include a personalized message too.

How can I contact customer service?

You can email us, use our contact form, or message us on social media. We typically respond within 24 hours, often much sooner!

Do you have a physical store?

We're currently online-only, but we do participate in local craft fairs and markets. Follow us on social media for event announcements!

How did you get into making earrings?

It started as a hobby in 2018 when our founder Sarah was inspired by watching Christmas Vacation! What began as a creative outlet became a passion for creating unique, conversation-starting jewelry.

Still Have Questions?

We're here to help! If you didn't find the answer you were looking for, please don't hesitate to reach out.
