After Each Wear

  • Gentle Wipe: Use a soft, lint-free cloth to gently remove any oils, makeup, or dirt
  • Check Hardware: Ensure ear wires are secure and not bent
  • Proper Storage: Store in their original pouch or jewelry box immediately

üöø Water and Moisture Protection

When to Remove Your Earrings

üöø Showering & Bathing

Always remove before getting in the shower or bath. Steam and soap can damage the protective coating and wood.

üèä Swimming

Pool chemicals and salt water can severely damage both the wood and metal components. Remove before any water activities.

üí¶ Exercise & Sports

Sweat can affect the finish over time. Remove before workouts, yoga, or any activity that causes perspiration.

üßñ‚Äç‚ôÄÔ∏è Spa Treatments

Remove before facials, massages, or any beauty treatments involving oils, creams, or steam.

üßΩ Deep Cleaning Guide

Weekly Deep Clean (If Worn Regularly)

  1. Prepare: Use a soft, slightly damp cloth (not soaking wet)
  2. Clean Gently: Wipe the wooden surface carefully, avoiding excessive moisture
  3. Dry Immediately: Use a dry cloth to remove all moisture right away
  4. Air Dry: Let earrings air dry completely before storing (about 30 minutes)
  5. Polish Hardware: Use a jewelry polishing cloth on metal components if needed

Stain Removal

Makeup Stains

Use a makeup wipe or gentle makeup remover on a cotton swab. Clean immediately with a damp cloth, then dry thoroughly.

Oil/Lotion Residue

Mild dish soap on a slightly damp cloth can help. Rinse with a clean damp cloth and dry immediately.

Stubborn Marks

Contact us for specific advice rather than using harsh chemicals that could damage the finish.

üì¶ Proper Storage

Daily Storage

  • Individual Pouches: Store each pair in its own soft pouch to prevent scratching
  • Jewelry Box: Use a jewelry box with separate compartments
  • Avoid Tangling: Don't throw multiple pairs together in one container
  • Room Temperature: Store in a cool, dry place away from heat sources

Long-Term Storage

For seasonal pieces like Christmas or Halloween earrings:

  • Clean First: Always clean thoroughly before long-term storage
  • Moisture Control: Include silica gel packets in storage container
  • Dark Storage: Keep away from direct sunlight and UV exposure
  • Annual Check: Inspect stored earrings once a year and clean if needed

üå°Ô∏è Environmental Factors

Temperature Considerations

  • Avoid Extreme Heat: Don't leave in hot cars or near heating vents
  • Prevent Cold Shock: Allow earrings to reach room temperature before handling if stored in cold areas
  • Humidity Control: Avoid storing in bathrooms or other high-humidity areas

UV Protection

  • Sunlight Exposure: Prolonged direct sunlight can fade colors over time
  • Display Cases: If displaying earrings, use UV-protective glass
  • Window Storage: Avoid storing near windows with direct sun exposure

üîß Hardware Maintenance

Ear Wire Care

  • Gentle Handling: Don't pull or twist ear wires roughly
  • Shape Maintenance: Gently reshape if bent, but don't over-manipulate
  • Tarnish Prevention: Wipe metal components with anti-tarnish cloth occasionally

When to Replace Hardware

Contact us if you notice:

  • Bent or damaged ear wires
  • Signs of metal fatigue or cracking
  • Loose connections between wood and hardware
  • Discoloration that doesn't clean off

üÜò Troubleshooting Common Issues

Issue: Colors Look Dull

Cause: Natural wear and oil buildup

Solution: Gentle cleaning with slightly damp cloth, followed by thorough drying

Issue: Wood Feels Rough

Cause: Moisture exposure or normal wear

Solution: Contact us - this may require professional refinishing

Issue: Paint Chipping

Cause: Impact damage or excessive wear

Solution: Contact us for repair options - we often can touch up minor chips

Issue: Ear Wire Discoloration

Cause: Natural oxidation or contact with chemicals

Solution: Try jewelry polishing cloth first, then contact us for replacement if needed

üí° Pro Tips for Longevity

üîÑ Rotate Your Collection

Don't wear the same pair every day. Rotating your earrings prevents excessive wear on any single pair.

üìÖ Seasonal Care

Give seasonal pieces (like Christmas earrings) extra attention when storing them away for most of the year.

üìù Keep Records

Note purchase dates and any maintenance performed. This helps track when professional care might be needed.

üéÅ Gift Care Kits

When giving earrings as gifts, include a soft cloth and care instructions to help the recipient maintain them properly.
