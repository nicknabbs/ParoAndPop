Processing Time

  • In-Stock Items: 1-2 business days
  • Custom Orders: 2-3 weeks
  • Holiday Seasons: May take an additional 1-2 days due to high volume
  • Weekend Orders: Placed Friday evening through Sunday will begin processing Monday

Geographic Coverage

Domestic Shipping (USA): We ship to all 50 states, including Alaska and Hawaii. PO Boxes and military APO/FPO addresses are accepted.

International Shipping: Currently not available, but we're working on it! International customers can use forwarding services.

Holiday Shipping Deadlines

To ensure delivery by major holidays, please order by:

  • Christmas: December 20th for standard shipping, December 22nd for express
  • Valentine's Day: February 10th for standard shipping, February 12th for express
  • Mother's Day: May 5th for standard shipping, May 8th for express

Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on the carrier's website. If you don't receive tracking info within 3 business days of placing your order, please contact us.

Shipping Issues

Lost Packages

If tracking shows your package as delivered but you haven't received it, please check with neighbors and your local post office. If the package cannot be located after 7 days, we'll send a replacement at no charge.

Damaged in Transit

We package everything carefully, but sometimes accidents happen. If your order arrives damaged, please contact us immediately with photos and we'll send a replacement right away.

Wrong Address

Please double-check your shipping address before completing your order. If you need to change the address after ordering but before shipping, contact us immediately. Address changes after shipping may incur additional fees.

Gift Orders

Sending a gift? We offer:

  • Free gift wrapping with personalized message cards
  • Gift receipts without pricing information
  • Direct shipping to the recipient's address
  • Special gift packaging available for an additional $3
